The Story Behind Anikara

Anikara was born from a deep love of Indian craft traditions and a desire to share them with the world. Block printing is one of India's oldest and most beautiful textile arts — each stamp a small act of creation, each design carrying centuries of heritage.

We believe that craft has the power to connect people — to their creativity, to each other, and to something much older and richer than our everyday lives.

Whether you join us at a workshop, commission a bespoke piece, or simply discover us through our shop, you become part of that story.

The Art of Block Printing

✦
🌿

1. The Block

Hand-carved wooden blocks depicting flowers, paisleys, and geometric patterns rooted in Rajasthani and Gujarati traditions.

🎨

2. The Ink

Natural, fabric-safe dyes — rich indigos, saffron yellows, earthy reds — applied to the block by hand.

✨

3. The Print

Each stamp is placed with care and pressed firmly. No two prints are exactly alike — that's the beauty of the hand.
